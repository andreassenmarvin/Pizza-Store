About us

Our Pizza is the modern foodie’s answer to a wholesome – and ridiculously delicious – hand-kneaded crust. We offer what is arguably the highest-quality product and selection in the category in all of us satisfying pizza lover taste buds with savory Italian flavor while also considering your health. We are committed to retail grocers, food service providers and their customers by providing innovative, unique, ready-to-bake pizza products that support current consumer trends surrounding healthier choices. The love and passion we have for the pizza we create represents the same love we have for our community. We believe in supporting the community that has supported us.
